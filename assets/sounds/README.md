# Sound Files

## Click Sound

To enable click sounds in exercise timers, add a `click.mp3` file to this directory.

### Requirements:
- **Format**: MP3 or WAV
- **Duration**: 50-100ms (very short)
- **Volume**: Low to moderate (not harsh)
- **Frequency**: Around 800-1200Hz (click/tick sound)
- **File size**: Keep it small (< 50KB)

### Where to find click sounds:
- [Freesound.org](https://freesound.org) - Search for "click" or "tick"
- [Zapsplat.com](https://zapsplat.com) - Free sound effects
- [SoundBible.com](https://soundbible.com) - Free sound effects

### Example search terms:
- "click"
- "tick"
- "soft click"
- "button click"
- "metronome tick"

Once you add `click.mp3` to this folder, the click sounds will automatically work in the exercise timers (plays at 2 and 1 seconds remaining).
