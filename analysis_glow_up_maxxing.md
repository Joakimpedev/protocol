## Design Patterns

### Color Palette
- **Background:** Pure black (#000000)
- **Primary accent:** Orange/red-orange (~#FF5733)
- **Positive:** Green (checkmarks, growth)
- **Negative:** Red (X marks, low scores)
- **Text:** White headlines, gray body
- **Special:** Full orange gradient background on Screen 16 only

### Layout Patterns
- Progress bar on question screens only (not value prop/mission screens)
- Orange CTA always at bottom
- Back arrow + Skip on all question screens
- Card-based multi-select with checkboxes
- Slider for subjective self-rating questions
- Orange/red gradient glow at top of question screens

### Unique Elements
- Hand-drawn circle on Screen 5 (authentic feel)
- Full orange screen break at Screen 16 (impossible to miss)
- Before/after split screen (Screen 20)
- Growth projection chart (Screen 22)
- Journey roadmap on paywall
- "Let's goooo!" casual CTA language

## Psychological Techniques

| Technique | Screens | Implementation |
|-----------|---------|---------------|
| **Curiosity Gap** | 2-3 | "37 other traits", "where you stand compared to others" |
| **Pain Amplification** | 9-15 | Systematic articulation of insecurities across 7 screens |
| **Social Comparison** | 3, 17 | Ranking against others, dating success hierarchy |
| **Social Proof** | 16 | 89% success rate, 105,439 specific user count |
| **Before/After** | 20 | Split screen mirrors admitted insecurities |
| **Future-Pacing** | 21-22 | Select desired outcomes, growth chart projection |
| **Anchoring** | 4, 23 | 68% score, weekly vs yearly pricing |
| **Commitment/Sunk Cost** | All | 22 screens of investment before paywall |
| **Empathy/Validation** | 12, 16 | Mission statement, "We get it." |
| **Scope Expansion** | 5, 17-19 | Looks → confidence → dating → career → mental health |
| **Reduced Perceived Effort** | 4, 16, 22 | 3-item checklist, "14 days", "Small Steps Big Changes" |
| **Confessional Framing** | 11 | "How would you honestly rate..." |

## Key Takeaways

1. **Longest onboarding (23 screens) = deepest emotional investment** - 3-5 minutes of articulating insecurities, goals, and desires before paywall
2. **Strategic question escalation** - Factual (gender, age) → Appearance concerns → Emotional (confidence, anxiety) → Dating/social → Future visualization
3. **Emotional rhythm** - Alternates between data collection and motivational/value screens to prevent fatigue
4. **Full orange screen break** (Screen 16) is brilliant visual disruption after dark introspective screens
5. **Before/After directly mirrors user's admitted insecurities** - feels personally designed
6. **Scope expansion** from looks to confidence, dating, career, mental health = higher perceived value
7. **Value-first** - 5 feature screens before asking for anything
8. **"We get it."** - two words that validate everything the user just revealed
9. **Growth chart** right before paywall = visual ROI projection
10. **94% price anchor** - Weekly at 199 NOK makes yearly at 11.52 NOK/week obvious choice
11. **No free trial** - relies entirely on 22-screen emotional investment for conversion
12. **Web purchase option** on Screen 1 - likely avoiding Apple's 30% cut
